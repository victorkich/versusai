Summary

Computer Engineer graduated from the Federal University of Santa Maria (UFSM), in Brazil. I am currently an Adjunct Professor of Robotics at Technological University of Uruguay - UTEC. I have a master degree in the robotics and automation field at Federal University of Rio Grande (FURG). In 2017, I joined the i9 Entrepreneurship League, an organization focused on fostering entrepreneurship in society, where I helped the organization of some events. In the same year, I also joined the Taura bots team where I started working with robotics and machine learning. Since then, I have worked on some other robotics projects, doing some publications and achieving some awards too. I am very interested in robotics, machine learning and entrepreneurship.
